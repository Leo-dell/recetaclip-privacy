Privacy Policy - RecetaClip

Last updated: September 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

RecetaClip collects the following information:

  • Recipes and Collections: The recipes you create and the collections you organize
  • Shared Links: URLs of recipe videos you share from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.
  • Language Preferences: Your preferred language setting
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (anonymous)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the recipe management service
  • Automatically generate thumbnails from recipe videos
  • Personalize your experience in the app
  • Show relevant ads (free version)
  • Process premium version purchases

3. App Permissions

RecetaClip requests the following permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: To extract thumbnails from shared recipe videos
  • INTERNET: To download recipe content and display ads
  • BILLING: To process premium version purchases

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With Google Play Services to process purchases
  • With Google AdMob to display ads (free version)
  • When required by law

5. Data Storage

Your recipes and collections are stored locally on your device. We do not send your recipes to external servers.

6. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
